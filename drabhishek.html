Dr. Abhishek Shrivastava

shri@iitg.ac.in

I am Abhishek Shrivastava, an Assistant Professor in the Department of Design (DoD) at Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati. In addition, I am actively associated with the CLST at IIT Guwahati.

My research interests include Voice User Interfaces (VUIs), Investigating Design Strategies for Users with varying Tech-readiness, Multimodal and Assistive User Interfaces.
